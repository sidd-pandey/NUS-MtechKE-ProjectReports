Dataset source: https://www.kaggle.com/c/data-science-bowl-2018

The basic proposal is to test/compare how data augmentation effects Nuclei Segementation task. 
The compared data augmentation techniques are Traditional(Flip, Rotate, Crop, Shear, etc.) versus GAN based synthetic image generation.
